At Your Door

The salon experience, without the salon trip.

With 7 years of professional grooming experience, Furtastic Mobile Dog Grooming brings a full-service pet spa directly to your driveway. We serve Mount Dora, FL and the surrounding communities with a level of care that no traditional salon can match.

Our self-contained grooming trailer is fully equipped with everything your pet needs — professional tub, high-velocity dryer, premium shampoos, and expert styling tools. Your dog gets our complete, undivided attention from start to finish. No other animals, no kennels, no rush.
