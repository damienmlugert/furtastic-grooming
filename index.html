What We Offer

Our Grooming Services

🛁

Bath & Brush

Full bath with premium shampoo and conditioner, blow-dry, and thorough brush-out. Leaves your pup fresh and fluffy.

✂️

Full Groom

Complete groom including bath, haircut styled to breed standard or your preference, nail trim, ear cleaning, and finishing.

💅

Nail Trim

Safe, precise nail trimming to keep your dog comfortable and your floors scratch-free. Quick and stress-free.

👂

Ear Cleaning

Gentle ear cleaning to remove buildup and help prevent infections. Included with full groom or available as add-on.

🐕

De-Shedding Treatment

Specialized treatment that removes loose undercoat to significantly reduce shedding and keep your home cleaner.

🐾

Puppy's First Groom

A gentle, positive introduction to grooming for puppies. We take extra care to make it a happy experience.
